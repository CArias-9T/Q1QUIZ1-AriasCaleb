Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse

After his journey in the previous movie, Miles gets to relax. But a new threat has apppeared, a strange threat, known as The Spot. He was once a scientist working for Kingpin, until an accident mutated him into a monster. He gained immense power, power which would disrupt the space-time continuium, which lead to Miles traveling across the multiverse in a goal to stop him, along with other Spidermen.
